# AI-Chips-Energy-Nuclear-Index-Dashboard-A-Power-BI-Driven-Analytical-Framework
Financial markets are complex, and investors need tools to analyze returns, volatility, and relationships between sectors to make informed decisions. A multi-page Power BI dashboard was built to provide insights into performance, risk, technical indicators, and correlations.

The expansion of AI applications ranging from machine learning to generative AI has significantly increased the need for specialized hardware such as GPUs, TPUs, and custom AI chips. These technologies require substantial computational power, which in turn drives high energy consumption.
Simultaneously, concerns about sustainability and carbon emissions have pushed industries to explore cleaner energy alternatives. Nuclear energy, with its low carbon footprint and high efficiency, is emerging as a viable solution to meet the energy demands of AI infrastructure.
This project aims to explore the intersection of:

•	AI chip industry growth 

•	Global energy consumption trends

•	Nuclear energy development 

Using Power BI, the project transforms raw datasets into a comprehensive analytical dashboard.

**Results and Findings**

The dashboard provides several important insights:

1. AI chip demand is growing exponentially, driven by AI adoption
2. Energy consumption is increasing in parallel, especially in data industries
3. Nuclear energy shows strong potential as a stable and scalable energy source
4. There is a clear correlation between AI infrastructure growth and the need for sustainable energy solutions
